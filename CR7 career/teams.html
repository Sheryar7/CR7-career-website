Teams Joined So Far

Cristiano Ronaldo as a professional footballer has played for 4 clubs and for his national team. He started his career in Sporting Lisbon where he made his debut. He just scored 5 goals in 31 appearances for the club.

Sir Alex Ferguson saw him playing and impressed him a lot so he signed him to Manchester United for 12 million euros or $19 million. Ronaldo scored 118 goals in 292 matches under Sir Alex Ferguson. He won his first Champions League trophy at Manchester United.

Then in 2009, he went for Real Madrid for 94 million euros or $131 million. In Real Madrid, he scored d 450 goals in 438 matches. He won four Champions League titles.

Then in 2018 he left Real Madrid and went for Juventus for $100 million.Ronaldo's stats at Juventus In 134 matches, Ronaldo scored a staggering 101 goals for Juventus. He scored 101 goals in 134 games with the Serie A giants.

Ronaldo made his official Al-Nassr debut on January 22 against Al-Ettifaq. It proved to be a rather flat occasion for the Portuguese, who failed to muster a single shot on target as he was handed the captain's armband immediately for his new team.
